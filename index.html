Introduction

I thrive on maximising business value from data assets.

Data is prolific across all business sectors. Every business will operate many applications and projects to manage and maximise value of this growing asset. Whatever the project, your business need or the size of your data repository, I am confident that I can add value to your technical or business teams delivering or utilising your data.

Profile

I specialise in analysing and understanding business processes from a data perspective and translating this into data solutions.

Over 25 years I have been delivering solutions where data integrity and quality has been paramount in producing consistently accurate results. My experience of data lifecycle processes has evolved through iteration of standards and practices as data volumes rapidly increased and technology adapted to cope, giving me a deep insight and understanding of what is now Data Management.

I have a hands-on approach to understanding and delivering data. My technical development background and specialisation in business focussed data exploitation, has fostered a style that enables me to work and communicate with all business and technical teams, as well as customers and suppliers. This is particularly valuable in conflict resolution and aligning expectations to capabilities, and vice versa.

I have successfully used canonical data modelling techniques to influence strategy and direction at project, product, and function levels. I also use these methods to great effect in identifying data relationships and flows for internal operations, external partners, customers and business planning.

I have had roles across all data management disciplines and am comfortable supporting analytical, development or operational functions within technical or business teams. I am as happy writing a SQL statement as I am a business requirement or use case. I will be delighted to attend an interview to elaborate on my experience and to discuss your contract, permanent or fractional data role.
